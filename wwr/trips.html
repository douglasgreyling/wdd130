Top Rated Trips

Wild Rapids Adventure

Embark on a heart-pounding journey through the untamed waters of the White River. This adrenaline-fueled expedition is perfect for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. Navigate through challenging rapids, surrounded by stunning landscapes and rugged terrain. With expert guides leading the way, conquer the river's twists and turns while experiencing the rush of white-water rafting at its finest.

Family Escape Rafting Retreat

Gather your loved ones for a memorable family getaway on the White River. This immersive experience offers the perfect blend of excitement and relaxation for all ages. Spend your days navigating gentle rapids, soaking up the sun, and bonding over shared adventures. With family-friendly guides and scenic picnic spots along the riverbanks, create lasting memories as you reconnect with nature and each other.

Weekend Wilderness Expedition

Dive into the ultimate outdoor adventure with our Weekend Wilderness Expedition. Escape the hustle and bustle of city life as you embark on a multi-day rafting journey through remote stretches of the White River. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of the wilderness, camping under the stars and waking up to the sounds of nature. Led by experienced guides, explore hidden waterways, discover secluded swimming holes, and forge unforgettable connections with fellow adventurers.
