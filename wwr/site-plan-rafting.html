Rafting Site Plan

Douglas Greyling

Overview

Purpose

  • Help potential customers find and learn about rafting trips, pricing, and availability.
  • Provide a platform for customers to book trips.
  • Build credibility and attract new customers.
  • Engage and educate customers with engaging content about rafting and adventure sports.

Audience

The audience includes individuals who are looking to book a rafting trip, families looking for a fun adventure, and groups of friends looking for an exciting activity. The audience may include people who are new to rafting and adventure sports, as well as experienced rafters looking for a new challenge. The audience may be local to the area or visiting from out of town and may need up-to-date information on when, or what, trips are available.
